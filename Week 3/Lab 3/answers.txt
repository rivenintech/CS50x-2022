sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: Faster than sort3 if the list is already sorted, but it's the slowest one in other cases.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: It's the fastest one (especially visible in list with larger amount of values).

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: Faster than sort1 in randomized and reversed list. Almost the same time in all types of list (sorted, reversed and random).
